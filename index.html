CFD FSI STAR‑CCM+ Overset Mesh

CFD Simulation of Fruit‑Fly Wing‑Beat Motion

Aim

Analyse the unsteady aerodynamics of a rigid fruit‑fly wing during hovering. Special emphasis on how the passive or active nature of pitch, stroke & elevation motions influences aerodynamic performance.

Methodology

  • Wingbeat trajectories reconstructed via Fourier series from high‑speed imaging
  • Transient STAR‑CCM+ solver (1/1000th wingbeat) with overset mesh
  • Stroke ↔ lift, elevation ↔ stability, pitch ↔ optimal angle‑of‑attack modelling

Key Results

  • Average lift ≈ 5.7 µN → Lift/Weight ≈ 1 (hovering ✅)
  • Pitch angle reversals + elevation peaks at stroke ends enable smooth AoA transitions
  • Moment hysteresis loops reveal active muscle contribution during pitch reversals
